Day 1: Using GNSS & Total Station for Control Point Recovery

In dense urban zones, GNSS signals often degrade due to multipath from high-rise buildings. To overcome this, we deployed a robotic total station aligned to a GNSS-derived temporary point. It improved accuracy and saved time.

Day 2: Embracing Drone Photogrammetry in Mapping

High-resolution drones are transforming survey workflows. RTK-enabled UAVs quickly deliver 3D data and orthophotos, integrating seamlessly into CAD/GIS software and improving safety in tough terrain.
