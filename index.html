Day 1: Using GNSS for Control Point Recovery

Today we encountered GNSS signal issues due to multipath errors from high-rise buildings. To overcome this, we used a total station connected to a temporary control point, established from a nearby known GNSS benchmark. Always consider combining technologies in difficult environments.

Day 2: Embracing New Surveying Technologies - Drone Photogrammetry

Modern surveying has seen a revolutionary shift with the integration of drone photogrammetry. These high-resolution UAVs can rapidly capture data over large areas with remarkable precision, drastically reducing man-hours and increasing site safety.

With RTK-enabled drones and post-processing platforms, surveyors now achieve centimeter-level accuracy — without setting foot on rough terrain.
